FIDO Q&A

  • Passwordless authentication
  • FIDO Alliance
  • WiSECURE – Enabler of Passwordless Authentication
Q1 What is WiSECURE's philosophy and role in passwordless authentication?

The mission of WiSECURE is to "enable enterprises to seamlessly deploy the FIDO2 authentication framework", and to provide consulting service for enterprises to acquire FIDO certificates.
Enterprises can choose to adopt security chips, microSD cards, USB security keys or our mobile app as the client-side FIDO2 authentication engine. We also provide FIDO2 authentication server as the enterprise-grade authentication core operating account binding and privileged access management.

 
Q2 In FIDO authentication framework, is a password or authentication secret required when an account is registered for the first time?

In enterprises, account binding, privileged access and permission setting are performed by IT-related personnel; on the consumer side, it is designed according to the service provider. If no password is used, it is recommended to have at least two non-password methods for authentication in case the user loses his security key (NIST SP 800-63A).

Q3 Besides access control, is audit logging performed?
Yes.
 
Q4 Regarding the FIDO service WiSECURE provides, is it possible to deploy it on cloud-based service/product?
The FIDO2 server solution provided by WiSECURE is deployed on premises and on the cloud as service.
 
Q5 Is passwordless authentication applicable to PAM (Privileged Access Management)?

Yes.
WiSECURE passwordless authentication can be applied to privileged account management.

 
Q6 Is FIDO applicable In a typical factory operated only through the intranet?

Yes. WiSECURE helps enterprises seamlessly integrate "devices, internal service cloud, FIDO2 authentication server" in a factory and provides tools and platforms for the factory to easily manage privileged accounts and authenticators.

 
Q7 In cloud-based verification, is session-based out-of-band authorization affordable or feasible?

FIDO authentication involves private or public network and does not support offline verification.

WiSECURE can build offline verification according to the application scenarios of the enterprises by using certificate issuance and verification mechanism.

 
Q8 Can FIDO provide two-factor authentication?

Yes.

 
Q9 Can passwordless authentication be integrated with cryptocurrency cold wallets?

Yes.
But the overall application scenario should be evaluated. Then passwordless authentication can be integrated accordingly.

 
Q10 Which unit does the pricing depend on, servers or accounts?

Both pricing models are available.

 
Q11 In WFH (Work from Home), can individuals purchase FIDO solutions? Is there any solution for individuals?

WiSECURE FIDO2 turnkey solution is mainly for enterprises. As to individual packages, a dealer or a reseller can buy our package and deploy it for individuals.
