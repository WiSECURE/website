What is Hardware Security Module (HSM) ?

A hardware security module (HSM) is a physical computing device that safeguards and manages cryptographic keys, performs encryption and decryption, digital signatures, strong authentication and other cryptographic functions. These modules traditionally come in the form of a plug-in card or an external device that attaches directly to a computer or network server.

Revolutionize HSM Market

WiSECURE HSMs aims for speedy deployment, high flexbility, adaptability and usability in vertical integration. Customers can save much time and put less effort. In this regard, it acts like a turnkey solution to deploy cryptographic service in specific use cases, meeting security requirements for data protection, authentication, etc.

Speedy deployment from clouds to ends

WiSECURE Technologies provides HSMs in a wide range of form factors, including PCIe cards, USB tokens and MicroSD cards intended for cloud-to-end deployment. The HSMs function as crypto platforms enabling customers to develop applications with less code to write.

Why WiSECURE HSM?

Best Practice

Looking for cryptographic key storage best practice? Compliant with the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) SP 800-57 guideline, cryptographic keys are managed strictly and never leave CC EAL 5+ certified seurity chips.

Modularization

WiSECURE insists on minimizing integration effort. With standardized HSMs sharing a unified crypto core and PKCS#11 API, deployment of HSMs is cost efficient and time saving.

Affordable Threshold

WiSECURE is positioned as a "crypto enabler", making HSM use affordable economically and technically. From techincal foundation, know-how and deployment, WiSECURE provides consulting service and deliver best practices for customers.

Specialized Algorithms

Years of development in cryptographic algorithms enable WiSECURE to customize algorithms to mitigate risks and to migrate to a new standard such as post-quantum cryptography. Algorithm upgradation does not require hardware replacement.

Speedy Integration

WiSECURE HSMs are highly adaptive. Enterprises can easily develop applications leveraging cryptographic functions performed by HSMs. The time and effort required for vertical integration is minimized, allowing for speedy time to market and cost efficiency.

Consultant service

As for those who do not have technical backgrounds, the exclusive fool-proof management tool allows no human error to occur.
