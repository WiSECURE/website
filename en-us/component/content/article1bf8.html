Former US Secretary of Homeland Security Janet Napolitano made a special visit to WiSECURE Technologies in Taiwan to learn about the country's top cybersecurity

Newsroom
May 12 2023

The 2023 Cybersecurity Conference grandly opened on May 9th, inviting the international guest Janet Napolitano, former US Secretary of Homeland Security, to visit.

Ms. Janet Napolitano, former US Secretary of Homeland Security and current President of the University of California, visited WiSECURE Technologies to gain insight into Taiwan's top cybersecurity research and development capabilities.

During the visit, Vince, the global marketing manager of WiSECURE Technologies, presented effective response measures and solutions to the "insider threat" arising from recent international cybersecurity accidents. Through Zero Trust Architecture and CMMC 2.0 compliant solutions, WiSECURE can prevent "insider threats" for enterprises, government, and even for national security purposes.

WiSECURE’s product line has acquired three FIDO2 certifications and FIPS 140 Level 3 certification, which impressed the former secretary as the standards were all set by the US, and the number of certified products is few.

WiSECURE’s FileAegis can prevent malicious file leakage within organizations. Its identity authentication mechanism not only acquired the government's zero-trust network certification, but it can also track file usage behavior to identify the source of file leaks, protecting corporate secrets and safeguarding sensitive government data.
